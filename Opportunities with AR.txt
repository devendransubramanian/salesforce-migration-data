Opportunities with AR 

  

This cross-object reports refers to use Opportunity columns and AR columns in the same report. 

  

For ex: 

Report link https://syncfusion.lightning.force.com/lightning/r/Report/00O2A000007Pw42UAC/view?queryScope=userFolders   

  

Sample Query : 

select t.id,pt.id, t.unique_external_reference_id, pt.cf_dt_order_id, pt.cf_outstanding_amount, 

    pt.cf_total_invoice_amount, pt.cf_last_ar_contact, pt.cf_collection_legal_status, 

    t."Opportunity Name", t."Account Name", pt.cf_ar_status, t.stage,pt."AR Rep" from public.ticket_detail t 

    LEFT Join public.child_ar_ticket_detail pt on t.id = pt.parentid 

    where t.closed_on between '2021-04-01' and '2021-05-01'   and t.stage like '%Closed Won%'
