Cases with Opportunity, Marketing Engagement with Opportunity  and Opportunity with related Opportunity 

  

This cross-object reports refers to use Opportunity columns and All Cases,Marketing Engagement  columns in the same report. 

  

Report Link : https://syncfusion.lightning.force.com/lightning/r/Report/00O3j000008BcvgEAC/view 

  

Cases With Opportunity: 

select t.cf_case_number,opp."Opportunity Name",t.cf_record_type from ticket_detail t left join related_opp_ticket_detail opp on opp.parentid=t.id 

  

where t.Category='Cases' and t.cf_record_type='Compliance' and opp.stage='Closed Won' and t.created_on > '2019-08-01' limit 100 

  

  

Marketing Engagement with Opportunity 

  

  

select t.subject,opp."Opportunity Name",t.cf_record_type from ticket_detail t left join related_opp_ticket_detail opp on opp.parentid=t.id 

  

where t.Category='Marketing Engagements' and t.cf_record_type='Bold Partner Outreach' and opp."Opportunity Name" is not null
